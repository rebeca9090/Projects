Explanation

This text in Romans is trying to tell us that, not only those who know God and follow His law will be considered righteous. Quite the opposite actually, Paul is telling us that, there is a lot of people that don't know about God but deep within them they end up following what the law says, because it's in their nature, kinda like having a moral compass or something similar to that. Therefore, those who do not know about God and His law, still can follow a right path because their hearts created a law for themselves. And if this law is similar to God's law, that shows everyone that not only their hearts are pure, but also the deep of their conscience.
